Events

Blood Donation

Blood Donation is like saving lives. Every year we Sinhgad College of Engineering’s National Service Scheme (SCOE NSS) arranges Blood Donation campaigns. Till now more than 1343 donors has donated blood through such drives organized by us. Our main objective behind conduct of these Blood Donation Camps is to promote and support voluntary blood donation by encouraging existing donors to give blood regularly and also encouraging new people to donate their blood on an unpaid basis. Also, to build wider public awareness of the need for regular blood donation throughout the year in order to maintain an adequate supply of blood for all patients.

Street Play

Education through entertainment can be easily done by Street Plays. The Main objective of street play is to create awareness among the general public about the problems that are plaguing the society and initiate a dialogue for change. From the beginning, SCOE NSS has performed many street plays at various places and events on different social subjects to spread awareness among the public. The plays that were performed talked about various issues- “Matdan Janajagruti”, and many more. SCOE NSS also participated in various events like Utkarsh State Level Social and Cultural Competition and many more, has also won various prizes and won hearts of people.

Cleanliness Drives

The importance of cleanliness in our lives cannot be denied. Maintaining a clean environment is for the health of all humans, as their health completely depends on the atmosphere. A bad environment is solely responsible for spoiling the health of the people around. Dirt and disease go together. Thus it is very important to maintain cleanliness for a healthy life. The team of SCOE NSS understands the importance of cleanliness and thus we conduct cleanliness drive every year. Till now we has conducted cleanliness drives at Rajgarh Fort, Sinhgad Fort, Taljai Hills and many a times in our college campus and area around it. Thus, contributing a small part towards keeping the environment clean.

Traffic Management

Traffic Management refers to the combination of measures that serve to preserve traffic capacity and improve the security, safety and reliability of the overall road transport system. As a responsible citizen we conduct Traffic management activity every year. Its main objective is to reduce road accidents, to provide adequate warning of temporary changes in road conditions, reduce congestion, improves traffic flows on primary and distributor roads. The main purpose of this activity is to help People in reducing traffic jams and also in saving the lives of people.

Tree Plantation

Step out of your house and we guarantee you won't stay away from the trees, unless you definitely live in the desert. From pine and oak, to willow and ash, we are surrounded by a wide variety of species. Our National Service Scheme unit performs tree plantation at least two times in a year and have planted around 4500 trees till now. We National Service Scheme’ volunteers are involved in every step of the reforestation process, each Rs.10 donation covers the seed collection, growing, planting and protection, guaranteeing a new forest to help biodiversity and tackle climate change.

Awareness Campaigns

We the SCOE NSS unit with the moto "NOT ME BUT YOU" arranges awareness programs like Organ Donation Awareness, Cancer Awareness, Road Safety and Traffic Awareness and many more for the society, breaks away the myths, especially to recognize the resources available and use them to address the needs of society. Social Awareness provides more of an accepting and comfortable society for those silent sufferers who are too afraid of being expressed in the society, We mostly reach out to remote areas where people actually need awareness. People get a helping hand in their day-to-day life by such awareness programs.

check Dam

We SCOE NSS unit conduct this check dam camp every year. Constructing Continuous Contour Trenches (CCT) reduces the soil loss and also helps in increase in water percolation. Every year we selects a village with semi-arid environment near Pune. CCT helps that village in regeneration of Ground water and hence ultimately reducing the water scarcity in the region. Till now, we have constructed 12 Continuous Contour Trenches in the villages nearby. Thus, contributing our small part in saving water and soil loss and ultimately bringing positive change in many lives of villagers.

Yoga Day

Yoga is an invaluable gift of India's ancient tradition. It embodies unity of mind and body; thought and action; restraint and fulfilment. The art of practicing yoga helps in controlling an individual's mind, body and soul. Every year International Yoga day is celebrated on 21st June. We SCOE NSS team also celebrates this day with great enthusiasm and performs various Asana. The main objective of celebrating it is to inculcate the habit of Yoga in the minds of volunteers and also to promote the benefits of doing it.

Winter Camp

Every year SCOE NSS organized a 7 days winter camp for village near Pune. SCOE NSS unit had done number of works during winter camp for overall development of villages. Selection of village is often based on the problems it faces and implementing solutions to it. In the winter camp, our team conducts number of awareness programs and activities for villagers. Four villages has been developed during such a winter camp by SCOE NSS unit. The main motive of conducting winter camp in village is the development of the village. Hence, work related to it is held in the village.These 7 days always makes a great impact on villager’s as well as Volunteer’s lives.

Seedball Making

Every year we SCOE NSS organizes Seedball Making activity. Volunteers makes such seedballs with the help of seeds and fertilizer. Then these seedballs being planted into the soil. This is one of the effective ways of afforestation. When nourished by rains, the seeds may germinate and grow into a tree. We SCOE NSS volunteers have made and planted more than 13500 seedballs till now.

Flood Relief Donation Drives

“Giving is not just about donating, it is about making a difference that helps humanity”. We SCOE NSS volunteers always stand behind our country for making a difference so that more people can involve in human livelihood. We have provided a helping hand to Maharashtra flood victims from 26th July 2021 to 1st August 2021, Kerala flood victims in the year 2018 and Kolhapur flood victims in the year 2019.The unit planned to provide them with aid through different channels by mobilizing funds and various relief materials. They started from ground level by collecting various daily goods which were requested from the campsites through the volunteer networks across different camps.
